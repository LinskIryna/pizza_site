Pizza neapolitan

The genuine Neapolitan pizza is made with only a few simple ingredients. It is prepared in only two variations — marinara, the basic Neapolitan pizza topped with a tomato sauce flavored with garlic, oregano, and extra virgin olive oil, and Margherita, which has a topping of tomatoes, mozzarella cheese (or, fior di latte), grated hard cheese, extra virgin olive oil, and a few fresh basil leaves.

Tomato
